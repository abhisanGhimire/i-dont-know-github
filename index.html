STEM at camp

At SET School (600 Central St, Holliston), our vacation camps follow the same rhythm as our published schedule: STEM academics in the morning—LEGO robotics, hands-on labs, coding, and experiments—and themed afternoons where kids explore animation, kitchen chemistry, circuits and invention, history and art, storytelling, and imaginative play. Days run 9 AM–4 PM with curiosity up front and fun woven through every block—plus sibling discounts and extended-day options for families who need them.

At camp, kids build real knowledge and skills across Science, Engineering, Technology, and other subjects—ideas, habits, and hands-on know-how that sit alongside the STEM core and show up in every week of April and summer at SET School.
