April vacation — April 21–24

No camp Monday (Marathon). Tuition: $360 for the full 4-day week, $100/day, $60 half-day.

Morning

Robotics: Build and program robots using LEGO MINDSTORMS. Add motors and sensors, then run a maze, shoot a basket, or try your own ideas.

Kitchen Chemistry: Science with everyday kitchen ingredients — chemical reactions, mixtures, and properties of matter from our own homes.

Afternoon

Summer vacation

Each week includes morning robotics plus a specialty camp; afternoons feature the theme listed below.

June 29 – July 3

Morning — Robotics: LEGO MINDSTORMS builds — motors, sensors, maze, basket shoot, or sumo-bot battle.

Morning — Atomic Cooking: Cooking is chemistry — changes when fire meets food, plus fun snacks.

Afternoon — Electric Boss: Electricity, wires, batteries, motors, LEDs, and circuits — build your own electric creations.

July 6 – 10

Morning — Robotics: LEGO MINDSTORMS — maze, basket, and more.

Morning — Med School Specialist: How the body works, what causes disease, and how specialists help hospitals care for patients.

Afternoon — Molecule Master: Mix compounds, test acids and bases, make slime, and explore chemistry hands-on.

July 13 – 17

Morning — Robotics: LEGO MINDSTORMS builds and challenges.

Morning — Ancient Art: Paper, paint, clay bowls, prehistoric tools, simple instruments — blending science, art, and history.

Afternoon — Secret Agent Academy: Codes, disguises, locks, spy gadgets, and laser “security” challenges.

July 20 – 24

Morning — Robotics: LEGO MINDSTORMS — your inventions and games.

Morning — Pokemania: Origins of species, ecology and physics of the Pokémon world, crafts, and play.

Afternoon — Animation: Stop-motion storytelling with clay, LEGOs, cutouts, and software.

July 27 – 31

Morning — Robotics: LEGO MINDSTORMS builds and challenges.

Morning — Not an Insect: “Bugs” that aren’t insects — sort them out and meet fascinating creepy-crawlies.

Afternoon — Action Contraptions: Build with cans, cardboard, wood, and more; tougher challenges for older campers.

August 3 – 7

Morning — Robotics: LEGO MINDSTORMS — arenas and games.

Morning — Medieval Makerlab: Shields, simple machines, castle engineering, and medieval tech without electricity.

Afternoon — Dungeons & Dragons: Heroic fantasy adventures using D&D rules — explore, battle, cast spells.

August 10 – 14

Morning — Robotics: LEGO MINDSTORMS builds and challenges.

Morning — School of Wizardry

Afternoon — Dungeons & Dragons: Continued heroic quests and cooperative storytelling.
